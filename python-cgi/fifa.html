FIFA 2016-2017 Player Stats

A complete record of statistics for all FIFA players

   The data set I used for this assignment holds personal information and professional statistics for every FIFA player in the previous 2016-2017 season. It lists facts about each player, such as age and nationality, and a long list of performance statistics, including personal fitness and position-based categories. These stats are given numbers out of 100.
